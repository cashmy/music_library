### Style Notes:

### Modal & Table Animation
- Create:  From the Right
- Review:  From the Left (Data exists)
- Update:  From the Left (Data exists)
- Delete:  From the Top

### Colors
- Create: Blue
- Review: White or Theme
- Update: Green
- Delete: Red
